<objective>
Delete the now-redundant per-page username and `onboarding_complete → /onboarding/schedule` redirects from all 8 `(tabs)` pages, while surgically preserving the auth guard (`!profile → /login`) and every functional non-onboarding redirect (cycle gender gate, u/me final redirect, groups membership redirect). After this plan, the layout gate (plan 03-02) is the single source of truth (D-03) and ONB-02 is fully satisfied.

Purpose: This completes the gate centralization. Leaving any per-page username/onboarding_complete redirect would create two sources of truth and could re-introduce the wizard bounce that D-02 removes. The work is mechanical line deletion with exact ranges pre-resolved in PATTERNS.md, but the preservation rules (Pitfalls 3 and 4) are load-bearing: blanket-deleting "all redirects" would break the cycle gender gate and the u/me redirect, and removing `if (!profile) redirect('/login')` would trip strict-null errors.

Output: 8 modified page files; no behavior change for username-set, setup-complete users; setup-incomplete users now stay in the app.
</objective>
