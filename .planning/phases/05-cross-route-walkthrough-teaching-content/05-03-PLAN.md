<objective>
Build the dashboard onboarding UX surfaces: a 4-item "getting started" checklist (UX-01), a "Start your first pact" empty-state CTA fallback for users who skip the tour (UX-02), the `data-tour="gym"` anchor (TEACH-01 reveal target), and the dashboard RSC wiring that feeds both.

**Resolves the load-bearing open decision (PATTERNS.md "Open Decision"):** the checklist needs per-key completion, but the frozen `useTour()` does NOT expose `completedSteps` and D-08/canonical_refs forbid extending `TourValue`. **Resolution (option 1 — recommended):** the dashboard RSC calls `getOnboardingProgress()` (already request-cached and used by the `(tabs)` layout — zero extra DB round trip) and passes `completed_steps` to `GettingStartedChecklist` as a `completedSteps: string[]` prop. The checklist is a presentation component over that prop — no context change, no extra client fetch.

Purpose: These are the standalone-discoverable onboarding affordances that work even when the coachmark engine is dismissed; they share the dashboard file so they ship as one plan (no file conflict with Plans 01/02/04).
Output: Two new client components and the extended dashboard RSC.
</objective>
